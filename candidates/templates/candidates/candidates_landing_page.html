How HireSpot Works

Here’s how to get started with HireSpot: Don’t worry if you can’t finish your profile or credentials right away. Click 'Finish Later' to complete the form later time. Required fields are marked with red asterisks (*).

Register

Congratulations on starting your new journey! Begin by signing up with your basic information. If all goes smoothly, you’ll be redirected to the login page to access your account and continue.

Login

Log in with the credentials you just created. Once entered correctly, you’ll be redirected to your profile page to complete your details and continue the process.

Complete Profile

Complete your profile details to help us match you with the best job opportunities. Accurate and thorough information ensures more relevant matches, so fill in as many fields as you can.

Add Credential

Once your profile is complete, add your qualifications, education, and work experience. Providing more details helps us match you with the best job opportunities. Use 'Add More' to include additional entries.
